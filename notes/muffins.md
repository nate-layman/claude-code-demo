# Tiller Belt Replacement

When replacing tiller tines belt:
1. Remove belt guard
2. Loosen idler pulley
3. Remove old belt
4. Install new belt around pulleys
5. Adjust tension
6. Replace guard

Belt part #: 954-04195

# Blueberry Muffins

2 cups flour
2 eggs
1/2 cup sugar
1/2 cup milk
1/4 cup oil
2 tsp baking powder
1 cup blueberries

Mix dry ingredients. Add wet ingredients. Fold in blueberries. Bake 400F for 20 min.
