# Lawnmower Carburetor Cleaning

Spark plug gap: 0.030"
Oil change every 50 hours
Air filter - check before each use

## Troubleshooting
- Won't start: check fuel, spark plug
- Runs rough: clean carburetor
- Replace fuel filter annually
