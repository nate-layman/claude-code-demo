# Pizza Dough

3 cups flour
1 packet yeast
1 cup warm water
2 tbsp olive oil
1 tsp sugar
1 tsp salt

Mix, knead, let rise 1 hour. Roll out and top as desired.

# Margherita Pizza

Pizza dough
Tomato sauce
Fresh mozzarella
Basil
Olive oil

Bake at 475F for 12-15 minutes.
