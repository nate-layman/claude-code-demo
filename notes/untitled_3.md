# Pancakes

2 cups flour
2 eggs
1.5 cups milk
2 tbsp sugar
2 tsp baking powder
pinch salt

Mix dry ingredients. Add wet ingredients. Cook on griddle.

# Waffles

Same as pancakes but add 1/4 cup melted butter. Cook in waffle iron.

# French Toast

Bread
3 eggs
1/2 cup milk
cinnamon
vanilla

Dip bread in egg mixture, fry until golden.
